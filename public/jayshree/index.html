Chapter VI · The Making

The Art of the Craft

01

Design & Sketch

Every piece begins as a dream on paper. Our artisans sketch intricate motifs inspired by temple carvings, nature, and Maharashtra's royal history.

02

Casting & Moulding

Metal is melted, cast, and carefully shaped. Each primary structure passes through 14 quality checkpoints before moving forward.

03

Stone Setting

Kundan, polki, and glass stones are hand-set with precision. This stage alone can take a full day for a single intricate necklace.

04

The Final Polish

Buffed to a mirror gold finish, quality inspected, and lovingly packaged. Only then does it leave our hands.
